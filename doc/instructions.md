# User Study Instructions: Feedback Interpretation & Analysis

Thank you for participating in this academic study. This project evaluates how researchers (like you) interpret and classify academic feedback within a research-proposal developmental cycle.

## Study Goal
The purpose of this study is to compare human interpretation of advisor-student feedback with and without the assistance of an AI-driven "Feedback Agent."

---

## 1. Setup Phase
When you first open the application, you will see a **Welcome Screen**:
1.  **Tester Number**: Enter your assigned participant ID (e.g., `001`, `005`).
2.  **Agent Assist**: 
    -   If your researcher told you to use the assistance tool, ensure this is toggled **ON**.
    -   If you are in the control group, toggle this **OFF**.
3.  Click **Start Study**.

## 2. Reviewing the Conversation
The interface is divided into two main panels:
-   **Left Panel (File Viewer)**: Displays research proposal PDFs. Click on file links in the conversation to view `v1.pdf`, `v2.pdf`, etc.
-   **Right Panel (Conversation)**: Displays the dialogue history between the "Advisor" and the "Student."

Please read the conversation carefully to understand the context of the feedback being given.

## 3. The Analysis Flow

### Mode A: Agent Assist (Assisted Group)
If you have Agent Assist enabled, you will follow these steps for each round:
1.  **Start Analysis**: Click the button once you've read the conversation. The AI will extract keywords and generate two interpretations.
2.  **Dual Interpretations (Step B)**: You will see "Task-Level" vs. "Process-Level" interpretations. Choose the one you believe is the **most primary factor** (ignoring secondary issues).
3.  **Your Reasoning (Step C)**: Explain *why* you chose that level in the text area.
4.  **Consistency Check (Step D)**: The AI will analyze your reasoning against the context and highlight parts it finds consistent or inconsistent.
5.  **Final Decision**: Provide your definitive choice and a **Confidence Score** (1 to 5).

### Mode B: Manual (Control Group)
If you have Agent Assist disabled:
1.  Review the conversation and relevant PDF files.
2.  Go directly to the **Final Decision** section at the bottom.
3.  Select your definitive choice (Task vs. Process) and provide a **Confidence Score**.

## 4. Submitting & Next Steps
-   Once you provide your final decision and score, click **Submit Final Results**.
-   After a success message, click **Next Conversation** to proceed to the next feedback round.
-   The study is complete when you've finished all available rounds for the current project.

---

### Important Notes
-   **Task-Level Feedback**: Focuses on *what* needs to be fixed in the project (e.g., "Add more data," "Fix this bug").
-   **Process-Level Feedback**: Focuses on *how* the researcher thinks or works (e.g., "You need to narrow your scope," "You should follow a different methodology").
-   **Privacy**: Your data is indexed by your Tester Number and stored securely for research purposes.

*If you encounter any technical issues, please notify the research administrator.*
